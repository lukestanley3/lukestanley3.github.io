geothermal energy

Geothermal energy is a form of clean and sustainable energy that uses the heat from the earth. Geothermal energy can extract energy from shallow ground to hot water and hot rock found a few kilometers below the earth's surface and even can go deeper to find magma rocks(extremely hot rocks). Geothermal energy is so useful because there is a lot of heat energy under the earth's surface just waiting to be tapped into. Geothermal energy can be converted to a lot of things, but is most useful when converted to electricity. It is a growing power source especially among schools in the states that are using it to power the air conditioning and heating. The largest geothermal power plant has the ability to power over 30 000 homes. If we expand this energy source it could turn into a massive source of clean energy for people around the world. Geothermal energy is an extremely cheap form of energy that would long term save and exponential amount of money for everyone. Geothermal energy is the future of clean energy because of how endless of a source it is and its affordability.
