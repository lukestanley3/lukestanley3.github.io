biomass energy

Biomass energy is a sustainable and very affordable clean energy source. Biomass is an organic material that comes from plants and animals. Biomass contains stored energy from the sun. Plants get energy from the sun during photosynthesis and then when biomass is burned the chemical energy in biomass is released as heat. Biomass energy is an extremely good alternative for fossil fuels because it is cheap and there is an endless source to create the energy. Biomass energy has many upsides such as it is clean and renewable, very cheap, and easily available; however it does have a done side. Biomass energy is not very strong source of energy. By itself it is not very powerful, but when it is used as a cogeneration alongside another energy source it drastically improves the amount of energy being produced. Biomass energy is a crucial piece to having a future powered by renewable energy and a clean environment.
