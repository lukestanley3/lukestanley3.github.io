fossil fuels

Fossil fuels are natural materials that are found in the earth. The majority of our energy sources are fossil fuels. Fossil fuels are unsustainable sources of fuel, eventually we will run out of theses sources such as: oil, coal, natural gas, and other fossil fuels. Not only are fossil fuels not sustainable and soon to be gone, but most of them have a negative affect on our environment. Drilling and mining, the two main ways to obtain fossil fuels have a very costly effect on the surrounding environment. There is also surface mining which destroys ecosystems and jeopardises wild life. Another huge environmental issue with fossil fuels and oil in particular, is the danger of transporting them. The past has shown that oil spill are extremely devastating to the environment. The risk of transporting oil is too large. We need to ditch fossil fuels before it's too late.
