clean energy

wind energy

The most common form of harnessing wind energy is wind turbines. Wind turbines are an incredibly useful tool for gathering clean energy. Wind turbines convert the kinetic energy of the wind into mechanical energy that can be used to power all types of things. Wind turbines are becoming increasingly more popular among large companies as a clean energy source for manufacturing. More wind energy has been built in Canada in the last 10 years then any other form of electric generator. Currently wind energy supplies 6 percent of Canada's electricity demands. Wind energy generates enough power to power over three million Canadian homes. If we continue to expand our number of wind turbines producing energy that number could grow exponentially. Wind energy is becoming extremely popular in all countries around the world. For example Canada invested approximately 1 billion dollars into wind turbines. This decision is a good choice environmentally and financially for Canada’s future.
